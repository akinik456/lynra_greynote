flowchart TD

A[App Start] --> B{Pattern exists?}

B -->|No| C[Onboarding]
C --> D[initializeSecurity]
D --> E[Generate secureSalt]
E --> F[Generate dbSalt]
F --> G[Generate rawMasterKey]
G --> H[User creates pattern]
H --> I[deriveKey with pattern + secureSalt]
I --> J[Wrap MasterKey]
J --> K[Store wrappedMasterKey]
K --> L[Go to Vault]

B -->|Yes| M[Unlock Screen]
M --> N[User enters pattern]
N --> O[deriveKey with pattern + secureSalt]
O --> P[unwrapMasterKey]
P --> Q[masterKey restored]
Q --> R[deriveDbKey with masterKey + dbSalt]
R --> S[Open SQLCipher DB]
Q --> T[derivePayloadKey]
T --> U[Cache payloadKey in session]
S --> V[Load collections]
V --> W[Load vault rows]
W --> X[Decrypt payload with payloadKey]
X --> Y[Show Vault UI]

Y --> Z[Create or Edit Item]
Z --> AA[Build payload JSON]
AA --> AB[Encrypt payload with payloadKey]
AB --> AC[Save encrypted payload into DB]

Y --> AD[Export Backup]
AD --> AE[Ask export PIN]
AE --> AF[Read DB rows]
AF --> AG[Decrypt payloads with payloadKey]
AG --> AH[Build portable JSON]
AH --> AI[deriveBackupKey with pattern + exportPIN + backupSalt]
AI --> AJ[Encrypt blob]
AJ --> AK[Save backup file]

Y --> AL[Import Backup]
AL --> AM[Pick backup file]
AM --> AN[Ask export PIN]
AN --> AO[deriveBackupKey with pattern + exportPIN + backupSalt]
AO --> AP[Decrypt blob]
AP --> AQ[Parse imported JSON]
AQ --> AR[Map collection by name]
AR --> AS[Assign new item IDs]
AS --> AT[Encrypt with current payloadKey]
AT --> AU[Insert into current DB]
AU --> AV[Refresh UI]

Y --> AW[App goes background]
AW --> AX[Auto Lock]
AX --> AY[Close SQLCipher DB]
AY --> AZ[Clear unlocked state]
AZ --> M
